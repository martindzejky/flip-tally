# Flip Tally

> Keep score, not paper.

Brand kit for Flip Tally. Pairs with `DESIGN.md` (visuals) and `README.md` (product).

## Strategy

### Overview

Flip Tally is a pocket score pad for Flip&nbsp;7 and other round-based table games. You
sit down, type in who's playing, and after each round you tap in everyone's points.
It adds up the totals and shows who's ahead. No setup, no account, no internet — the
game lives in your browser and installs to your home screen like an app. It replaces
the scrap of paper that always goes missing halfway through game night.

### Positioning

A single-purpose tool, not a game and not a rules engine. It does not deal cards,
enforce Flip&nbsp;7's rules, or track stats over time. It is the fastest path from "round's
over" to "here's the score." Unofficial companion — not affiliated with The&nbsp;Op.

### Personality

Archetype: the friendly scorekeeper. Playful, bright, and tactile, but never in the
way. Every control feels like a chunky game piece you want to tap. It is fun to look
at and boring to operate — in the best way.

What it is: warm, quick, legible, confident.
What it is not: corporate, clever, cluttered, gamified-for-its-own-sake.

### Promise

Type names, tap scores, see the winner. It works on the first try, on any phone,
even with no signal.

### Guardrails

Tone is upbeat and plain. Never hype, never cute-to-the-point-of-annoying. Litmus
test: would this feel right shouted across a noisy table mid-game? If not, cut it.

## Voice

### Identity

We are the scorekeeper at the table — cheerful, fast, and never the center of
attention. Essence: bright and out of the way.

### Tagline & Slogans

- Primary: **Keep score, not paper.**
- Alt: _Tap. Tally. Win._
- Empty state: _No players yet — add the table._

### Message Pillars

- **Fast** — fewer taps than finding a pen.
- **Offline** — installs to your phone, runs with no signal.
- **Private** — scores never leave your device; no accounts.
- **Playful** — looks like the games it scores.

### Phrases

- "Add the table."
- "Round's in."
- "Who's ahead?"
- "New game, same crew."

### Tonal Rules

Lowercase, friendly microcopy. Short imperatives. No exclamation-mark spam (one is
plenty, and only on a win). Numbers are the loudest thing on screen, not the words.

| Say                   | Never say                   |
| --------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Add player            | Onboard a participant       |
| Round's in            | Submission successful       |
| Start playing         | Get started on your journey |
| Scores stay on device | Privacy-first cloud sync    |
| New game              | Reset experience            |

## Visual

Retro-playful board-game look: warm cream canvas, teal / coral / gold accents, and a
rainbow of per-player colors borrowed from Flip&nbsp;7's number cards. Rounded, chunky
"game-piece" shapes and a friendly rounded display face. See `DESIGN.md` for tokens,
type, layout, and shape rules.

## Name

"Flip Tally" — _flip_ nods to Flip&nbsp;7's flip-a-card mechanic; _tally_ is all the app
does: add up the rounds.
